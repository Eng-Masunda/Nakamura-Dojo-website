Our Instructors...

Branch Chief / Country Representative: Shihan Tawanda Mufundisi

Shihan Tawanda Mufundisi is a 5th Dan Kyokushin Instructor. He is the branch chief and country representative of IKO Nakamura Kyokushinkaikan in Zimbabwe. He was the undisputed Kyokushinkaikan SA Lightweight Champion from 2007 to 2010. He's a two-time All-Africa Champion for 2008 and 2010, World Lightweight Bronze Medalist at the 4th Kyokushinkaikan Matsushima World Tournament 2014, Mr 50-Men kumite in 2009.Shihan Tawanda is also a caring husband and father of three.

Technical Director: Sensei Thomas Chiwomba

Sensei Thomas Chiwomba is a 2nd Dan Kyokushin instructor. He is the Technical Director of IKO Nakamura Kyokushinkaikan Zimbabwe. He has won multiple National middle-weight championships. He participated in the Len Barnes tournaments in SA, also he is the western Cape Province middle-weight bronze medalist. He participated in Shihan Mufundisi's 50-men Kumite. Currently he is the national coach of IKO Nakamura.

Head Instructor: Sensei Malvern Chomusora

He is a 2nd Dan Kyokushin Instructor and also the head Instructor for IKO Nakamura.He has won multiple National and regional middle-weight championships. He is currently the team captain of IKO Nakamura. He has participated in multiple international seminars and he is also the athletes representative of IKO Nakamura. He works together with the Technical Director and one of the senior instructors, Sensei Edmond Munyukwi in organising teams and events for the organisation.
